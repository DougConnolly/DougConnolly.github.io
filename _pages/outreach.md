## Math and Science Outreach events with the OIST Community

Since 2024 I have participated in a number of events on Okinawa that are focused on introducing middle school and high school students to the world of science and mathematics. This has involved making presentations and leading group activities. One of the most satisfying parts of living and working in Japan has been working with young students. It also provided me with an opportunity to communicate core mathematics concepts in both English and Japanese. Some of the resources that were used are linked below.
 
* Graph Local Equivalence Classes. Science Day Gunma 2025--The World of Quantum Mechanics, science outreach presentation at Gunma University, October 2025

 
* Mathematics with Games. Onna x OIST Children's School of Science 2025, presentation at OIST, August 2024, and August 2025


* Thinking Mathematically: Searching for Patterns! (Japanese language version) Science outreach presentations for middle school students visiting OIST, 2025
